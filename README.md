# #73 Plus Five

A minimalist progressive timer designed to help you beat your yesterday's self. Specifically crafted for static exercises like planks, where "just 5 more seconds" makes all the difference.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Open and start. No settings, no distractions.
- **Progressive Load**: Automatically suggests "Last Record + 5 seconds" as your next goal.
- **Mental Friction Zero**: Designed to eliminate the hesitation of "how long should I go today?".

## Features
- **Auto-Suggest**: Calculates your next target based on your previous successful session.
- **Smart Adjust**: Minimalist `[-5s]` and `[+5s]` buttons for quick tuning.
- **High Visibility**: Large, bold timer display designed for low-angle viewing (on the floor).
- **Audio Feedback**: Distinct beeps to signal completion without needing to look at the screen.
- **Persistent State**: Remembers your current goal even if the browser is refreshed.
- **Hidden Calibration**: Tap the main timer digits to fine-tune by +1s.

## Design System
- **Background**: #fff
- **Main Color**: #333
- **Border Radius**: 15px (All elements)
- **Visuals**: Dynamic font scaling for single-digit countdowns to maximize focus.

## Privacy & Performance
- **No Login**: Start immediately.
- **No Tracking**: Your progress is stored only in your browser (LocalStorage).
- **No Ads**: Pure focus on your training.
- **Browser Only**: Works on any device, optimized for low-spec mobile/Safari.
